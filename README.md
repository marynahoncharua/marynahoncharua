# 👋 Hi, I’m Maryna Honchar — Manual QA Tester & Business Analyst

I’m a Manual QA Tester and Business Analyst with practical experience in testing web and mobile applications, reviewing business logic, and helping development teams build clearer and more reliable digital products.

Since 2017, I’ve been working part-time with Web Art Work, contributing to real projects across websites, CRM systems, admin panels, mobile applications, and internal business tools.

My main focus is to understand how a product should work from both the business and user side, then carefully test whether the application behaves as expected.

## 🔍 What I do

I help teams improve product quality through:

* Manual testing of web and mobile applications
* Functional testing
* Regression testing
* UI and UX review
* Business logic validation
* Bug reporting and issue tracking
* User flow testing
* Requirement review
* Test cases and checklist preparation
* Communication between business and development teams

## 🧩 Business analysis

As a Business Analyst, I help clarify product requirements and make sure the team understands what needs to be built.

I can help with:

* Understanding business needs
* Writing and improving task descriptions
* Preparing user stories and acceptance criteria
* Reviewing product flows
* Finding unclear or missing requirements
* Checking whether the feature matches the expected result
* Helping developers understand the user and business context

## 🧪 Quality assurance

My QA approach is focused on real user behavior, clear communication, and practical feedback.

I test products on different devices and environments, including:

* Android phones
* iPhone / iOS devices
* Windows computers
* macOS computers
* Different browsers and screen sizes

I pay attention not only to bugs, but also to usability, confusing flows, missing details, layout issues, and places where the product may not feel clear for the user.
